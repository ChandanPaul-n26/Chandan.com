Frequently Asked Questions

Type in the search box to filter FAQs in real time.

How do I reset my password?

Go to the account settings, choose "Password", and follow the reset flow. If you can't access your email, contact support.

How do I create an account?

Click the Sign Up button on the homepage and fill in the required details. Verify your email to complete setup.

How do I request a refund?

Open the Orders page, select the order and choose Request Refund. Our team will review within 3-5 business days.

Does the site work on mobile?

Yes, our website is responsive and supports modern mobile browsers.
