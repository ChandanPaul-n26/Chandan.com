How it works

This simulator computes projectile motion using standard kinematic equations (no air resistance):

  • x(t) = v0 * cos(angle) * t
  • y(t) = v0 * sin(angle) * t - 0.5 * g * t²

Units: meters and seconds. The canvas uses a scaling factor to display meters as pixels.
